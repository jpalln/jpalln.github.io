What is AutoPay?

  • Passenger credit card details are stored in the app for automatic payment at the end of each trip.
  • When you ENTER FARE from the meter in your app, Hailo immediately charges the passenger's card.
  • Tip is added automatically after you enter the fare.
  • Receipts are emailed to you and your passenger at the end of the trip.

What are the benefits of AutoPay?

  • No more skipped fares, payment is guaranteed.
  • Move on to your next passenger right away.

Payment details

  • Payment is processed the same as credit cards—no extra fees.
  • Occasionally, your taxi's GPS may experience a signal issue and will not tell you payment has been processed. Don't worry: You will be paid. Do not accept payment in the cab.
